About the Project

This Eagle Scout project aims to assemble 100 individual first aid kits for elderly residents at an independent living facility. Each resident will receive a personal kit providing immediate access to essential medical supplies.


These kits promote safety, preparedness, and independence. All donated items and funds go directly toward purchasing first aid materials.
